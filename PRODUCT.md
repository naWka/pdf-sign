product

## Product purpose
Sign & Fill PDF — a Chrome MV3 extension that signs and fills any PDF **entirely in the browser, locally**. The file never leaves the device, no account, no upload. Free to start; a one-time lifetime unlock removes the export gate. Market: US/EU.

The wedge, checked against every decision:
**Sign & fill any PDF right in your browser. No upload. No account. Your files never leave your computer.**

Three pillars, visible in the UI itself (not only the store listing):
1. **Privacy** — the file stays on the device, works offline.
2. **Zero friction** — no sign-up, no upload, a signature in ~10 seconds.
3. **Forever** — one payment instead of an Adobe/Smallpdf subscription.

## Users
Freelancers, self-employed / sole traders, realtors, accountants, HR, small business in US/EU who regularly sign contracts and forms and refuse to hand sensitive documents to a third-party server. Secondary: people googling "how to sign a pdf without adobe."

They are not power users of design software. They want the document signed and downloaded, trusting that nothing leaked, then to close the tab.

## Tone
Calm, trustworthy, precise, fast. The confidence of a good desk tool, not the noise of a SaaS dashboard. Speaks plainly: "Your file stays on this device." No hype, no growth-hacky nudges. Privacy is stated as fact, not sold as fear.

## Anti-references
- **Adobe Acrobat** — bloated, modal-heavy, corporate red, account-walled.
- **DocuSign** — enterprise workflow, team seats, audit trails, corporate navy.
- **Smallpdf / iLovePDF** — cloud upload as the default, subscription treadmill, busy tool-grid homepages.
- Generic SaaS: gradient hero-metric dashboards, teal "secure" clichés, blue-and-white productivity template.

We are a narrow, local, personal tool. The narrowness is deliberate, not an unfinished product.

## Strategic principles
- **The file never touches a server.** Verifiable in the Network tab. This IS the product; the UI should make it felt.
- **No remote code** (MV3): pdf.js, pdf-lib, fonts all bundled.
- **Minimum permissions.** Every extra permission hurts install conversion and store review.
- **No accounts inside the product.** License by key, not by login.
- **Offline-first.** Core function and the paid unlock both work with no network.

## Out of scope (deliberately)
Accounts, cloud storage, sync, team features, share-to-sign, audit trails, legal e-sign compliance guarantees, CRM integrations, mobile, editing the PDF's own text, OCR, subscriptions.
